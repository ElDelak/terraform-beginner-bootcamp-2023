Tunisian Lablabi

Lablabi is a popular Tunisian dish. It’s a chickpeas soup served as a breakfast street food during cold days. It became very popular in the late decades. Other than its nutritious value, it is a must eat in winter.

Preparation
12 min
Cooking
60 min
People
2
Difficulty
easy
ingredient
  • 2 cups of Chickpeas, rinsed and soaked overnight
  • 4 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1/2 lemon
  • salt, pepper,cumin powder, harissa, capers
  • olives, olive oil, tuna (canned), pickled pepper
  • 2 minute-boiled egg for each serving
  • bread, torn into medium pieces
preparation
  1. Rinse the chickpeas and let them soak overnight in 3 liters of water.
  2. Put the chickpeas with its water in a large sauce pan.
  3. Add the garlic cloves, 2 tbsp of olive oil and the half lemon and cook for about 1 hour and a half or 2 hours, until the chickpeas are tender.
  4. Serve the soup in bowls where you individually add the seasonings, bread and egg. Mix and eat.
